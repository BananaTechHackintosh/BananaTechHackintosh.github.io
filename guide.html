Key steps (in plain HTML)

  • Prerequisites: Windows Vista or newer, 16 GB USB, reliable internet; ethernet recommended for installer.
  • Check compatibility: Run OpCore Simplify to confirm the highest supported macOS for your CPU, GPU and chipset.
  • Build EFI: Gather kexts, configure config.plist with ProperTree, and clean unused entries.
  • Create installer: Use OpenCorePkg’s macrecovery to fetch macOS, then write the USB with Rufus.
  • Install: Boot from USB, complete Setup Assistant, then mount and copy the working EFI to the internal disk.
  • Post-install: Enable Wi-Fi if supported by your chipset and kexts; verify sleep, audio and iGPU/dGPU.
  • Backup: Keep a copy of your known-good EFI on the USB in case of future updates.
