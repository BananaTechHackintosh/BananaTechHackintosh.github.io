About Banana Tech

Banana Tech was created to make the Hackintosh process easier for beginners and enthusiasts alike. Our step-by-step guide walks you through setting up macOS on compatible PCs using OpenCore, with clear instructions and troubleshooting tips.

We believe in open knowledge and accessible tools. Whether you’re building a Hackintosh for work, school, or personal projects, our goal is to help you get up and running with as little hassle as possible.
